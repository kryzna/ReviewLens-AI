Full product spec: `../../specs/SPEC.md`

## Mandate

Build a web portal where an analyst ingests reviews for a product from Trustpilot, Apple App Store, or Google Play (or uploads CSV/JSONL), sees an ingestion summary, and Q&As over that data with a scope-guarded LLM. Public URL, no auth, deployable to Fly.io.
